# Literature Survey

## Paper 1: Temporal Text Classification with Large Language Models (2026)
* **Goal:** Evaluate LLMs on automatic dating of texts (Temporal Text Classification).
* **Dataset:** CLMET (Corpus of Late Modern English Texts), sourced from Project Gutenberg.
* **Timeline:** 1710–1920 (Divided into three 70-year periods).
* **Key Benchmark:** The SVM baseline using TF-IDF vectors achieved a Macro F1-score of **0.78** on 100-token inputs.
